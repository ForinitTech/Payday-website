FAQ

  • 1. How To Play On Payday App?
  • 2. What Is Fantasy Sports?
  • 3. What Are Credits?
  • 4. Can I Edit My Team After I Create It?
  • 5. Which Type Of Contest Can I Join?

Log in with your mobile number to register on Payday App. Follow the instructions as you set up your player profile. Once done, follow the steps to participate in this game of mind and thrills!
1. Select the match
2. Pay entry fee and join contests
3. Put your skills to test and create your Fantasy team
4. Check the real-time leaderboard while you watch the match LIVE

Fantasy sports is a strategy-based online sports game where you can create a virtual team of real players, playing in live matches worldwide. You earn points and win cash prizes based on the performances of these players in actual matches.

Credits are the cost of a player. In-form and star players typically cost more credits, while inexperienced or non-regular players cost less.

You can edit your team as many times as you like before the card deadline. Go to My Teams on the match page to check all the teams you have created.

Select your favourite sport, click on a match from that sport, create your Payday and join any public, private, head-to-head or mega contests. You, can also choose to join paid contests by paying the contest entry amount or hone your skills by entering practice contests before you compete with others.
