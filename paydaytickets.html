What is a Payday Ticket?

A Payday Ticket is a special reward for achieving first place in designated contests. By winning first in one of these contests, you earn a Payday Ticket, which allows you to enter an upcoming, payday-supported contest with one free entry slot.

How Payday Tickets Work:

  1. Earning a Payday Ticket:

    2. Compete in a designated payday ticket-eligible contest. If you secure the first position, you’ll be awarded a payday ticket for a future match.

  2. Using Your Payday Ticket:

    3. You can redeem the payday ticket in an upcoming payday-supported contest, where it grants you one free slot, making your first entry free.

  3. Track Your Tickets:

    4. View your available payday tickets and eligible contests in the “Payday Tickets” section in the Account tab. This makes it easy to see where and when you can redeem them for maximum benefit.
