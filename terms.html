      • Basketball: Points for scoring, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks; negative points for turnovers, fouls, etc.
      • Football: Yards gained, touchdowns, interceptions, sacks, field goals, etc.
      • Soccer: Goals, assists, clean sheets, saves, tackles, cards, minutes played, etc.
